skating

I started skating when I was 8 years old. A few of the coaches I've worked with are: Tiffany Chin, Oksana Grishuk, Karen Kwan, and Rafael Arutyunyan. By July 2016, I had passed every single skating test and was finally at the highest level - senior. I then auditioned for and was selected to join the Los Angeles Ice Theater's Senior Team. I also competed solo in Senior Solo Ice Dance.
